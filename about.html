About the Artist

Hello, I'm Divyansha.

I'm a self-taught artist based in India with a deep love for colour, texture, and the quiet beauty that surrounds us every day. I founded Huevia as a space to share what I create with the world.

I work primarily in acrylics, with a particular love for charcoal and graphite sketching. I enjoy the boldness and colour of acrylic paintings just as much as the quiet detail and contrast you can capture in a charcoal or graphite piece.
