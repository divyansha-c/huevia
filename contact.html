Shipping

Delivery & Packaging

Every painting is packed with the same care as if I were gifting it to a friend.

📦

How I Pack

Each painting is wrapped in glassine paper, padded with bubble wrap, and placed in a rigid cardboard box — no corners get bent!

🚚

Delivery Time

Mini paintings: 3–5 days. Small–Medium: 5–7 days. Large paintings: 7–10 business days. All via reputed couriers.

Shipping Charges

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Are all paintings originals?
+

Yes — every single painting in my shop is an original, one-of-a-kind piece. I don't sell prints. Each artwork comes with a certificate of authenticity signed by me.

How do I buy a painting?
+

Simply click "Inquire" on any painting in the gallery and it'll open a WhatsApp message to me directly. Tell me you'd like to purchase it, confirm your address, and I'll share payment details. It's that simple!

Can I see more photos before buying?
+

Absolutely. Just message me the title of the painting and I'll send you detailed close-up shots, texture photos, and photos in natural light so you can see it properly before committing.

How long does a commission take?
+

Mini paintings take 2–3 weeks from confirmation to delivery. Medium–large commissions take 3–5 weeks. Large statement pieces can take up to 8 weeks. I'll give you an accurate timeline when we discuss your order.

Can I follow the painting of my commission?
+

Yes! I always share a work-in-progress photo once the painting is around 50–60% done so you can confirm you love the direction before I finish it.
