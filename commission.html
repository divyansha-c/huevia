The Process

How commissions work

From your first message to a finished painting on your wall — here's what to expect.

1

Share Your Vision

Fill in the inquiry form or message me on WhatsApp with your idea, preferred size, and any reference images.

2

We Discuss & Quote

I'll respond within 1–2 days with a custom quote, timeline, and any questions to refine the concept.

3

I Paint Your Piece

Once you confirm with a 50% advance, I begin the painting. I share a WIP photo for your approval.

4

Delivered to Your Door

After final payment, your painting is carefully packed and shipped to you with a certificate of authenticity.
